Electronic Financials Users’ Circle (EUC), Inc.

About EUC

EUC started in 2006

Initially, it is an association of users and enthusiasts of the e-NGAS.

Non-Stock And Non-Profit Association

October 15, 2009, it was registered with the Securities and Exchange Commission as a non-stock and non-profit association.

Public Financial Management Professionals

To date, it has evolved to an association of public financial management professionals who utilized information and communications technology as a tool for productivity, transparency and accountability.

EUC’s primary purposes are to:
  1. Promote and maintain high standards in profession relating to government financial management;
  2. Enable the accounting information technology and other related professions to discharge responsibilities pertaining to government financial management more effectively;
  3. Elevate the standards of government financial management education and profession through the use of modern tools such as Information and Communication Technology;
  4. Promote relevant programs and projects of the government as a whole and those of the professions related to government financial management;
  5. Develop among its members high ideals of competence, ethical conduct, integrity and civil consciousness;
  6. Guard against the practice of the related professions by unauthorized persons or entities; and,
  7. Foster cordial, harmonious and fruitful relations among its members.
